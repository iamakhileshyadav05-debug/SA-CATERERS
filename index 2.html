Our Story

A Legacy of Taste & Tradition

SA Caterers was born in 1998 from a simple yet powerful belief held by our founder, Lalit Yadav: that extraordinary food, served with grace, transforms any occasion into a cherished memory. Founded in Delhi NCR, we have spent over two decades mastering the art of celebration cuisine.

From the first wedding we catered in 1998 to the thousands of events that followed — each plate we serve carries our promise of quality, hygiene and hospitality. Our team blends time-honoured recipes with contemporary presentation, creating experiences that guests remember long after the last course.

Today, SA Caterers stands as one of Delhi NCR's most trusted catering institutions — chosen by families, corporations and dignitaries alike.

Meet Our Founder

LY Founder
The man who started it all

Lalit Yadav

Founder & Director  ·  SA Caterers  ·  Est. 1998
"Food is not just nourishment — it is memory, it is love, it is the soul of every celebration. Every dish we serve carries that belief."

Lalit Yadav founded SA Caterers in 1998 with a singular dream: to bring restaurant-quality cuisine and five-star hospitality to the celebrations of everyday families across Delhi NCR. Starting from humble beginnings, he built the company plate by plate, event by event, earning trust through unwavering commitment to taste and service.

With over 26 years of hands-on experience in the culinary and hospitality industry, Lalit has personally overseen thousands of weddings, corporate dinners and social gatherings. His deep understanding of flavour, presentation and guest experience is the foundation upon which SA Caterers stands today.

Under his leadership, SA Caterers has grown into one of Delhi NCR's most respected catering institutions — a name synonymous with quality, elegance and tradition.
