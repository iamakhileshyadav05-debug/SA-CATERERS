Our Story

A Legacy of Taste & Tradition

SA Caterers was born in 1998 from a simple yet powerful belief held by our founder, Lalit Yadav: that extraordinary food, served with grace, transforms any occasion into a cherished memory. Founded in Delhi NCR, we have spent over two decades mastering the art of celebration cuisine.

From the first wedding we catered in 1998 to the thousands of events that followed — each plate we serve carries our promise of quality, hygiene and hospitality. Our team blends time-honoured recipes with contemporary presentation, creating experiences that guests remember long after the last course.

Today, SA Caterers stands as one of Delhi NCR's most trusted catering institutions — chosen by families, corporations and dignitaries alike. Our exclusive partnership with Khemchand Ramkishan Halwai, Chandni Chowk ensures that the finest traditional Indian sweets grace every celebration we cater.

Our Heritage Sweets Unit

In Association with Khemchand Ramkishan Halwai

Bringing the timeless taste of Chandni Chowk's most legendary halwai to your celebration.

Khemchand Ramkishan
Halwai  ·  Chandni Chowk, Old Delhi

SA Caterers is proud to operate an exclusive traditional mithai and sweets unit in association with Khemchand Ramkishan Halwai — a legendary name in Chandni Chowk renowned for generations of authentic Indian confectionery.

This partnership brings the rich heritage of Old Delhi's finest mithai directly to your wedding and event catering — from classic motichoor laddoos and kaju katli to sohan halwa, barfi and seasonal specialities crafted using original family recipes.

When you book SA Caterers, you get not just a catering team — you get a legacy of sweet craftsmanship from the heart of Chandni Chowk served at your celebration.
